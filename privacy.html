The Observation

When you enter the MBOYI INDIVA construct, we gather specific data points: your digital footprint, browsing patterns, and interaction heatmaps. This is not surveillance in the vulgar sense, but rather the careful study of how our universe is navigated.

"We do not sell your identity. We study your presence to perfect the architecture of exclusivity."

Purpose & Usage

The information collected serves three purposes:

Refinement: Understanding which elements of our universe resonate most deeply allows us to calibrate the mystery.

Exclusivity: Maintaining lists of approved visitors and those who must remain outside the construct.

Logistics: Fulfilling orders of our physical artifacts—fashion, spirits, and transport—with precision.

Digital Traces (Cookies)

We employ minimal tracking technologies. Cookies serve as temporary keys, allowing your browser to remember your position within our universe. They expire when your session ends, leaving no permanent residue unless you opt into "Permanent Access" (our loyalty construct).

Third Party Involvement

Selected data is shared with:

• Payment Processors: Encrypted transaction handlers who never see your full identity.
• Logistics Partners: Courier services receive only delivery coordinates, never your complete profile.
• Legal Authorities: Only when compelled by legitimate judicial force.

Your Sovereignty

You retain the right to request the erasure of your data from our archives. However, understand that removal from our database constitutes permanent exile from the MBOYI INDIVA ecosystem. You will become invisible to us, and we to you.

To request data deletion, use the Direct Line with subject line "Request for Erasure".

Retention

We retain your data indefinitely, or until you request its destruction. This allows us to recognize returning visitors, maintaining the continuity of your narrative within our universe. Long-term observation reveals patterns; patterns reveal truth.
